Alternative to FoodChéri: what to replace it with?

FoodChéri now targets companies. Here are the best alternatives to FoodChéri to get healthy meals delivered at home or to cook fresh.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Alternatives

Looking for an alternative to FoodChéri for individuals? FoodChéri long appealed with its fresh dishes delivered at home. Today the service focuses on tasty lunches delivered to the office, in a corporate logic. To eat healthy at home, here is where to turn.

Alternative to FoodChéri: the quick recap

To replace FoodChéri at home, two options: ready-to-heat dishes (Seazon, Aussitot Bon, Kitchen Daily) if you do not want to cook, or a box to cook (Quitoque, Les Commis) for fresh produce in season. All offer flexible meal delivery, commitment-free, with a menu that changes each week. To compare these services, use our comparator of the best meal kit boxes.

FoodChéri for individuals: is it over?

The brand shifted its model to corporate catering, like Frichti. For home use, there are luckily solid alternatives, often more flexible on subscription and dish choice. The new market situation works in your favour: more choice, better on freshness and value.

Eating healthy and sustainable day to day

One of FoodChéri's strengths was its sustainable side and attention to healthy eating. Good news: the alternatives follow the same logic. Quitoque bets on local and season, Seazon on balanced meals, Kitchen Daily on dishes by a starred chef. You keep the quality and the sustainable spirit, while getting real delivery for individuals. Our verdict

The best alternative to FoodChéri depends on your appetite for cooking. For ready dishes, Seazon or Kitchen Daily. To cook fresh, Quitoque or Les Commis. For weight loss, Cheef. Either way, you regain a meal delivery built for individuals, which FoodChéri no longer really offers.

Does FoodChéri still deliver individuals?

The offer now leans toward companies and office lunch. For home, prefer Seazon, Aussitot Bon or Kitchen Daily.

What alternative to FoodChéri for healthy delivered meals?

Kitchen Daily and Cheef for the balanced side, Seazon for variety. All deliver fresh meals at home.

Is there a FoodChéri alternative to cook yourself?

Yes, Quitoque and Les Commis offer fresh products in season to cook, with guided recipes.

FoodChéri or a classic meal kit?

Since FoodChéri no longer targets individuals, a classic meal kit (Quitoque, Seazon) is now the best choice to eat well at home.
