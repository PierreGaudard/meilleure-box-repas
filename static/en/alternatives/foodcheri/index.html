FoodChéri for individuals: what to replace it with?

FoodChéri now targets companies. Here are the best alternatives to get healthy meals delivered at home or to cook fresh.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Alternatives
  3. FoodChéri for individuals: what to replace it with?

FoodChéri long appealed to individuals with its fresh meals delivered at home. Today the service focuses on tasty lunches delivered to the office, in a B2B logic. If you are looking to eat healthy at home, here is where to turn.

FoodChéri for individuals: is it over?

The brand shifted its model toward corporate catering. For home use, there are luckily solid alternatives, often more flexible on subscription and meal choice.

The best alternatives to FoodChéri

  • For ready-to-heat meals: Seazon and Aussitôt Bon, with a menu renewed weekly.
  • To eat balanced or lose weight: Cheef and Kitchen Daily.
  • To cook fresh yourself: Quitoque and Les Commis.

If you hesitate between cooking and ready meals, our meal kit comparison puts these services head to head.

Our advice

Define your need first: cook or reheat. Then compare the price per meal and the flexibility. Commitment-free services let you try without risk before settling on one box.

Frequently asked questions

Does FoodChéri still deliver individuals?

The offer now leans toward companies. For home, prefer Seazon, Aussitôt Bon or Kitchen Daily.

What alternative for healthy delivered meals?

Kitchen Daily and Cheef for the balanced side, Seazon for variety.
