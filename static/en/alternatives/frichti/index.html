Frichti has changed: what alternatives for your meals?

Frichti has refocused on companies. For individuals, here are the best alternatives for ready meals or a meal kit to cook.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Alternatives
  3. Frichti has changed: what alternatives for your meals?

You were looking to order from Frichti and you land on a B2B offer? That is normal. The brand, long known for meals and groceries delivered to individuals, has now refocused on the corporate canteen market. To cook or get meals delivered at home, you need to look elsewhere.

What changed at Frichti

Frichti no longer really offers a consumer service of delivered meals as before. The activity is shifting toward corporate catering. Good news: the meal kit market has never been so rich, and the alternatives are often better on freshness and price.

The best alternatives to Frichti

To cook fresh and seasonal without spending your evenings on it, Quitoque stays our reference. If you prefer ready meals, look at the ready-meal services in our comparison.

How to choose without getting it wrong

The simplest is still to compare services side by side on price, freshness and flexibility. Our meal kit comparator gives you the verdict in seconds, so the decision becomes obvious.

Frequently asked questions

Does Frichti still exist?

The brand still exists but now mainly targets companies. The consumer meal delivery as we knew it is no longer the focus.

What is the best alternative to Frichti?

To cook fresh, Quitoque or Les Commis. For ready-to-heat meals, Seazon or Aussitôt Bon.
