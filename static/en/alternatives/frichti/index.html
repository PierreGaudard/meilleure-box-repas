Alternative to Frichti: what to replace it with?

Frichti has refocused on companies. Here are the best alternatives to Frichti for meals delivered at home or a meal kit to cook.

By Benjamin Bourgoin
  1. Home
  2. Alternatives
  3. Alternative to Frichti: what to replace it with?

Looking for an alternative to Frichti? You now land on a corporate offer, and that is normal: the brand, long known for dishes and groceries delivered to individuals, has refocused on B2B. To cook or get meals delivered at home, you need to find something else. Good news: the alternatives are many, often better on freshness and price.

Alternative to Frichti: the quick recap

To replace Frichti for individuals, two paths: ready-to-heat dishes (Seazon, Aussitot Bon) if you do not want to cook, or a box to cook (Quitoque, Les Commis) for fresh produce to prepare yourself. All offer reliable home delivery and a commitment-free service. To compare, use our comparator of the best meal kit boxes.

What changed at Frichti

Frichti no longer really offers a meal delivery service to individuals as before. The activity shifted to corporate catering, with connected fridges in offices. For consumers, the good news is the meal kit market has never been so rich: you easily find better on freshness and French sourcing.

How to choose without getting it wrong

The simplest is to compare services side by side on price, freshness and flexibility. Ask first whether you want to cook or not, then check the delivery available at your address. Some services cover all of France, others only big cities. Our meal kit comparator gives the verdict in seconds, and our guide on how to choose your meal kit details the method. On budget, count less than a delivered restaurant dish, especially with a box to cook. The good reflexes: use a welcome offer, then adjust.

Our verdict

The best alternative to Frichti depends on your appetite for cooking. For ready dishes, Seazon or Aussitot Bon. To cook fresh and French, Quitoque or Les Commis. Either way, you will find better than the old Frichti offer on freshness, all delivered at home commitment-free.

Does Frichti still exist?

The brand still exists but now mainly targets companies. The meal delivery to individuals is no longer the focus.

What is the best alternative to Frichti?

To cook fresh, Quitoque or Les Commis. For ready dishes, Seazon or Aussitot Bon.

Is there a Frichti alternative delivered everywhere in France?

Yes. HelloFresh covers almost all of France, a practical alternative outside big cities.

Which Frichti alternative for office lunch?

The individual ready dishes of Seazon or Kitchen Daily are perfect for lunch, made to be reheated in minutes.
