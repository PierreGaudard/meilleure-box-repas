Alternative to Rutabago: the organic box and its replacements

Rutabago, a pioneer of the organic meal kit, joined Les Commis. Here are the best alternatives to Rutabago for an organic, seasonal meal kit.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team

Looking for an alternative to Rutabago? The brand made its mark as the 100% organic, responsible meal kit. If you try to subscribe today, you are redirected to Les Commis: Rutabago joined that group. The organic, seasonal spirit has not vanished, and several services take over to cook fresh and healthy.

Alternative to Rutabago: the quick recap

To replace Rutabago, two paths: Quitoque, which offers lots of seasonal products and real flexibility, or Les Commis, which took over part of the offer. If you mainly want raw fruit and vegetables, a grower basket does the job. All these options are commitment-free. To compare, use our comparator of the best meal kit boxes.

Why Rutabago redirects to Les Commis

Rutabago was integrated into Les Commis. In practice, the box to cook offer still exists, but under another banner. The organic, quality and responsible promise remains at Les Commis, with chef-signed recipes and ingredients delivered measured. To find a close experience, here are the best options.

An organic box, is it worth it?

An organic meal box guarantees ingredients from organic farming, often seasonal and more responsible. The price is a bit higher, but you gain in quality and traceability. To cook healthy only with good products, this type of box stays relevant, especially for people attentive to their diet. If budget is a criterion, see our guide on how much a meal kit costs, and to arbitrate by profile, follow how to choose your meal kit.

How to choose your alternative to Rutabago

Ask first whether you want to cook or not, then what you expect on organic and season. Letting go of a brand you liked is never pleasant, but the market now offers ways to get the same quality. Each option has its strengths: Quitoque for flexibility, Les Commis for heritage, Potager City for raw fruit and vegetables.

Our verdict

The best alternative to Rutabago is Quitoque for its freshness and flexibility, or Les Commis which took over part of the offer. Both let you cook healthy meals from quality ingredients, delivered to you commitment-free. The organic meal box keeps all its meaning, simply under other names.
