Rutabago: what happens to the organic box and its alternatives?

Rutabago, a pioneer of the organic meal kit, has joined Les Commis. Here are the best alternatives for an organic, seasonal meal kit.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Alternatives
  3. Rutabago: what happens to the organic box and its alternatives?

Rutabago made its mark as the 100% organic, committed meal kit. If you are trying to subscribe today, you will be redirected to Les Commis: the brand joined that group. The organic, seasonal spirit has not disappeared, and several services take over.

Why Rutabago redirects to Les Commis

Rutabago was integrated into Les Commis. In practice, the meal kit offer still exists, but under another banner. To find a close experience, here are the best options.

The best alternatives to Rutabago

For a meal kit with lots of seasonal produce and real flexibility, Quitoque ticks the right boxes. If you mainly want raw seasonal fruit and vegetables, look at grower baskets. Compare it all in our comparison.

Frequently asked questions

Does Rutabago still exist?

The brand joined Les Commis. Visiting the Rutabago site redirects you.

Which organic box instead of Rutabago?

Quitoque for seasonal sourcing and recipe choice, or Les Commis which took over part of the offer.
