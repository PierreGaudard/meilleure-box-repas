Which is the best alternative to HelloFresh?

Looking for a more premium alternative to HelloFresh? Our comparison of the best meal kits against HelloFresh: Quitoque leads, with scores and prices.

By Benjamin Bourgoin

HelloFresh made the meal kit mainstream in France, but many customers now look for a more premium alternative: fresher produce, less standardised recipes, more vegetarian choice or short supply chains. We ordered and tested the main boxes that rival HelloFresh to see which ones truly do better, at a comparable budget.

Why look for an alternative to HelloFresh

HelloFresh remains the best-known box, with the largest recipe catalogue and slick logistics delivering everywhere in France. But its industrial model has limits: sometimes standard produce, recipes that repeat from week to week, and a commercial commitment some find heavy. Those looking for a more premium meal kit turn to players that focus on freshness, season and short circuits.

The principle stays the same: meal baskets with measured ingredients and recipe cards delivered to your door each week, ordered in a few clicks. What changes between brands is the produce selection, taste, variety and price per basket. This is exactly where Quitoque takes the lead.

Quitoque: the best alternative to HelloFresh

Quitoque is the cook-yourself box that answers point by point the criticisms made of HelloFresh. Produce is fresher and more often seasonal, with a share of French and local ingredients, the menu makes real room for vegetarian, and new recipes truly vary each week. The basket is delivered everywhere in France, easy to order and to pause as needed. At a near-identical price, it is the best taste-freshness balance for anyone who wants to cook good produce without spending hours. See more in our Quitoque review.

Quitoque pros

  • Fresher produce and more seasonal recipes, with French and local sourcing.
  • Real vegetarian choice, wider than HelloFresh.
  • Genuinely varied recipes, less repetition.
  • Price comparable to HelloFresh at equal quantity.

Quitoque cons

  • Recipe catalogue a little smaller than the HelloFresh giant.
  • Delivery areas to check depending on the region.

Les Commis: the most premium alternative

Les Commis pushes the quality cursor even further, with crafted recipes and selected produce. It is the high-end alternative to HelloFresh, a step above on the gourmet side, for a slightly higher budget.

Les Commis pros

  • Carefully worked recipes, a more gourmet dimension.
  • Fine-quality produce.

Les Commis cons

  • Slightly higher price.
  • Fewer plans than HelloFresh.

Seazon, Aussitôt Bon, Kitchen Daily and Cheef: the no-cooking alternatives

If the whole idea is to stop cooking, these ready-meal boxes are real alternatives to HelloFresh. Seazon offers balanced meals ready to reheat, Aussitôt Bon focuses on home-style cooking delivered, Kitchen Daily on chef dishes, and Cheef on a weight-management angle. They do not play in the same category as the cook-yourself box, but they save busy weeks.

Which alternative to HelloFresh for your needs

Each profile has its ideal alternative. To save time while still cooking, Quitoque remains the strongest idea: a fresh basket delivered each week, new recipes and the act of cooking without the chore of shopping. For a more gourmet, crafted feel, Les Commis plays the chef-recipe card. For those who really have no time, Aussitôt Bon and Seazon deliver already-cooked meals, ready to reheat.

The right reflex is still to compare the produce selection and the price per basket before ordering. A slightly pricier box with better produce often works out the same once waste is avoided. That is where Quitoque stands out against HelloFresh.

How to choose your alternative to HelloFresh

Three questions settle it. Do you want to keep cooking or switch to ready meals? To cook better than HelloFresh, Quitoque stands out. Does produce quality matter more than catalogue size? Then Quitoque or Les Commis. And what budget per plate? At a near-equal price, Quitoque offers the best quality-freshness ratio. Which meal kit is more premium than HelloFresh?

Does an alternative to HelloFresh cost more?

Not necessarily. Quitoque sits at a price comparable to HelloFresh at equal quantity, while offering fresher produce. Ready-meal boxes such as Seazon or Cheef have a different price positioning, per plate, depending on the number of meals ordered.

Can you switch box without commitment?

Yes. Most alternatives to HelloFresh, including Quitoque, work without commitment: you can pause or cancel at any time. It is one of the criticisms of some competing offers, and an argument to try a more premium box risk-free.
