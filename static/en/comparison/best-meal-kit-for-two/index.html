Best meal kit for 2 people: which box to choose as a couple?

Which meal kit for 2 people should you choose? Our comparison for couples: right-sized portions, price per plate for two and varied recipes.

By Benjamin Bourgoin
  1. Home
  2. Comparison
  3. Best meal kit for 2 people

Cooking for two means the supermarket's oversized portions and the endless "what's for dinner". A meal kit sized for two fixes both at once. We ordered and cooked the main plans as a couple, paid for our own baskets, and looked at which ones really hold up for a household of two.

In short

Quitoque is our best meal kit for 2 people. Its plans start from two servings, with right-sized portions that really limit waste and genuine French quality. For two, the price per plate is a little higher than for four, which is true for every box (degressive pricing).

Why a meal kit when you are two

For two, you often toss half a bunch of vegetables or a pack of meat. A box measures the ingredients precisely, saves time on shopping and breaks the evening routine, all without thinking about the menu, with simple recipes and portions designed for a couple. It is the format where the comfort gain is the most obvious.

Quitoque: the ideal box to cook for two

Quitoque offers plans from 2 servings, with right-sized portions that genuinely limit waste. It is our best meal kit for a couple: 44 new recipes every week, two reworked by chef Philippe Etchebest, 100% seasonal vegetables at more than 80% French origin, and dishes ready in under 30 minutes. 90% of recipes score Nutri-Score A or B. You pick your number of recipes and enjoy a real home-cooked dinner without spending the whole evening at it. More in our Quitoque review.

Quitoque pros

  • Plans from 2 servings, right-sized portions, little waste.
  • French quality: seasonal vegetables, more than 80% French origin.
  • 44 recipes per week, ready in under 30 minutes.
  • Flexible, commitment-free plan, from EUR 3.63 per plate on larger formats.

Quitoque cons

  • For two, the price per plate is higher than for four (degressive pricing).
  • You cook yourself.
  • Delivery free only on the first order.

Les Commis: the gourmet couple's dinner

For a slightly more gourmet dinner for two, Les Commis are a safe bet. Original recipes signed by chefs, remarkable organic and French ingredients, and dishes ready in 20 minutes. It is a notch above on produce, with a slightly more premium positioning. For two, it is the right choice to turn a weeknight into a real moment. More in our Les Commis review.

Les Commis pros

  • Chef recipes, perfect for a couple's dinner.
  • Top-tier organic and French ingredients.
  • Recipes ready in 20 minutes.

Les Commis cons

  • More premium than Quitoque.
  • Slightly narrower menu.

HelloFresh: the widest choice for two

HelloFresh bets on volume: its catalogue is one of the widest on the market, ideal for a couple who like to vary without ever getting bored. The two-person plan is the standard entry level, with an aggressive entry price on the first order. The sourcing is less centred on season and French origin than Quitoque. More in our HelloFresh review.

HelloFresh pros

  • The biggest recipe catalogue on the market.
  • Generous welcome offer.
  • Varied formats by household.

HelloFresh cons

  • Less seasonal and less French sourcing.
  • Price climbs after the promo.

Seazon: ready meals for no-cook evenings

Not every evening is the same. Seazon delivers ready meals to reheat, perfect for two when no one feels like cooking. It is not a cook-yourself box, but it is a handy home delivery to get through a busy week. The downside is that you do not cook and the choice is more limited. More in our Seazon review.

Seazon pros

  • Meals ready to reheat, zero prep.
  • Handy for rushed evenings for two.
  • One delivery for several meals.

Seazon cons

  • You do not cook, these are ready meals.
  • Narrower menu.

Cheef: balanced ready meals

Cheef targets balance and weight management with calibrated ready meals, ready in a few minutes. For a couple watching their plate without cooking, it is an honest option, from EUR 4.90 per dish. The framework is a bit rigid (the programme guides the menus) and these are not raw products. More in our Cheef review.

Cheef pros

  • Calibrated balanced meals, ready in minutes.
  • Dietary guidance.
  • Accessible price per dish.

Cheef cons

  • You do not cook, not raw products.
  • The programme guides the menus, less freedom.

How much does a meal kit for 2 cost

The price of a box rests on degressive pricing: the more servings and recipes you order, the lower the price per plate. For two you are at the top of the range, often between EUR 4 and 7 per portion depending on the brand and plan. Welcome offers lower the first order. Also think of the real cost versus shopping: for two, a box's right-sized portions avoid the waste that is common when cooking small amounts. Frequently asked questions

What is the best meal kit for 2 people?

For a couple who want to cook with right-sized portions and quality, Quitoque leads, thanks to its plans from two servings, its seasonal vegetables and its French origin above 80%. Les Commis follows for a more gourmet dinner.

Is a meal kit for 2 worth it?

The price per plate is higher for two than for four, because pricing is degressive. But the right-sized portions avoid the waste common when cooking for two, which rebalances the budget versus shopping.

How many recipes per week for 2 people?

Most boxes let you choose, often 2 to 5 recipes per week, to adjust to your rhythm and the number of evenings you cook at home.

Are the portions of a box for 2 enough?

Yes, they are sized for two adults. For big appetites, adjust the number of recipes rather than the portions, or add a side.
