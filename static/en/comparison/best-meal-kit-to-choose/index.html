Which meal kit box should you choose?

Comparison of the best meal kit boxes of the moment. Price, freshness, recipe choice: how to find the service made for you.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Comparison
  3. Which meal kit box should you choose?

Choosing a meal kit quickly means comparing dozens of near-identical offers. Yet behind the marketing, the gaps are real on produce freshness, subscription flexibility and price per meal. Here is our read of the market to aim right.

The criteria that make the difference

  • Freshness and origin of the produce, ideally seasonal and local.
  • The breadth of recipe choice each week.
  • Subscription flexibility, pause included, commitment-free.

Our express ranking

On the balance between produce quality and ordering freedom, Quitoque comes out on top, thanks to baskets built around seasonal produce.

How to decide by profile

A busy household will favour simplicity and quick recipes. A budget-conscious family will first look at the real monthly cost of a meal kit. For more, our guide explains how to choose your box step by step.

Frequently asked questions

Is a meal kit cheaper than a restaurant meal?

Yes, by a lot. The cost per portion stays below a delivered dish, while offering fresh produce to cook yourself.

Can you pause your subscription?

Most serious services, Quitoque included, let you skip a week or pause without fees.
