Quitoque or HelloFresh: which meal kit to choose?

Quitoque or HelloFresh comparison: freshness, recipe choice, price and flexibility. We compare the two brands point by point to help you choose.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Comparison
  3. Quitoque or HelloFresh: which meal kit to choose?

Two names always come up about meal kits to cook: Quitoque and HelloFresh. One bets on fresh and local, the other on a global catalogue and an unbeatable entry price. To decide between Quitoque or HelloFresh, we tested both brands, paid for our baskets, and compared what really separates them once in the kitchen.

Quitoque or HelloFresh: the verdict in short

If your number one criterion is freshness and local, Quitoque is the best choice. If it is the entry price and catalogue variety, HelloFresh takes the lead. Both are flexible, commitment-free, with a pause possible anytime. To compare with all brands, use our comparator of the best meal kit boxes.

Freshness and origin of produce

This is where the difference is clearest. Quitoque offers fresh produce in season, with French sourcing and lots of organic. The ingredients are firm, the fresh ingredients well chosen, the local origin highlighted. HelloFresh offers correct but more standardised products. Advantage Quitoque on this criterion. See our Quitoque review.

Recipe choice and variety

On variety, HelloFresh strikes hard: its catalogue is one of the widest, renewed weekly, with a huge choice of dishes. Quitoque offers fewer references but well-thought simple recipes. If you want an immense choice, HelloFresh wins. If you prefer a tight but quality selection, Quitoque stays our reference. See our HelloFresh review.

Is price really decisive?

HelloFresh draws you in with a very aggressive welcome price. But once the promo passes, the gap narrows. Quitoque starts at EUR 3.83 per serving on big plans, practical on the budget for those who order in volume. To compare the real cost over time, promo included, read our guide on how much a meal kit costs. Price alone is not enough to make the right choice.

Convenience and flexibility day to day

On convenience, the two brands are equal: you build your plan, choose your recipes, receive measured ingredients with clear cards. Both are flexible and commitment-free, with pause, change and cancellation free. The customer service is responsive at both. The real difference is philosophy: fresh and local on one side, catalogue and entry price on the other.

Quitoque or HelloFresh by profile

  • You want to eat local and seasonal → Quitoque.
  • You are starting and want the lowest price → HelloFresh.
  • You want an immense recipe choice → HelloFresh.
  • You prioritise fresh ingredient quality → Quitoque.
  • Still unsure → test both with the promos, then keep the one that fits.

The advantages of each box

Quitoque: fresh produce in season via short circuits, clear recipe cards that simplify the little evening organisation, a controlled cost per recipe, and recyclable packaging.

HelloFresh: the biggest catalogue to simplify the choice, several plans and different formats by household, a generous welcome offer, and home delivery available almost everywhere.

Beyond the duel: what about Les Commis?

Reducing the market to a single opposition would be a shame. For more premium recipes signed by chefs, Les Commis play in the same league as Quitoque, with good fresh produce and solid logistics. A third option worth knowing. To compare all the two services and more, our comparator is the fastest tool.

Our verdict on this comparison

At the end of this comparison, Quitoque wins on freshness, local origin and the quality of fresh produce, making it the best choice for most households. HelloFresh keeps the edge on the entry price and variety. Choose depending on your priority criterion.

Frequently asked questions

Is Quitoque more expensive than HelloFresh?

In practice, the two are close. HelloFresh is often cheaper on the first order, then prices converge.

Quitoque or HelloFresh for eating local?

Quitoque, no contest, thanks to its seasonal fresh produce and French sourcing.

Which has the biggest recipe choice?

HelloFresh, whose catalogue offers the greatest variety of dishes.

Can you test both without commitment?

Yes. Both are flexible and commitment-free, so you can compare for yourself.

Quitoque or HelloFresh: which to choose in the end?

Choose by priority: Quitoque for fresh and local, HelloFresh for entry price and variety. For most, Quitoque is the best compromise.
