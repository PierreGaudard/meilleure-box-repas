How much does a meal kit cost per month?

Price per serving, delivery fees, promo codes: we break down the real monthly budget of a meal kit to avoid surprises.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Guides
  3. How much does a meal kit cost per month?

The price of a meal kit is never just the figure on the homepage. Between cost per serving, delivery and welcome promotions, the real monthly bill deserves a clear look. Here is how to estimate it without getting it wrong.

Price is calculated per serving

Most services charge per meal and per person. The more recipes and servings you order, the lower the unit cost. That is the number-one lever to bring the bill down.

The items to include

  • Price per serving: the most decisive.
  • Delivery fees: often free above a threshold.
  • Welcome promo code: strongly reduces the first month.

Beware of deals that look too good: the gap evens out once the discount is gone, as shown in our Quitoque vs HelloFresh comparison.

Cutting the bill without cutting quality

Limiting waste also lowers the real cost per plate. To pick the best value service, lean on our Quitoque review.

Frequently asked questions

Is delivery free?

Often free above a certain order amount, otherwise a few euros.

Is the first month representative?

No. Welcome promos skew the maths. Estimate your budget on the full price.
