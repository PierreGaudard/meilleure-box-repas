Frequently asked questions

What is the best meal kit box?

It depends on your priorities. In our comparison, Quitoque leads for freshness and flexibility, HelloFresh for choice and entry price, Seazon for convenience. The comparator helps you find the one that suits you.

Are there commitment-free meal kit boxes?

Yes. The main boxes (Quitoque, HelloFresh, Seazon) are commitment-free: you can skip a week, change your recipes or cancel at any time.

Which meal kit box is the cheapest?

HelloFresh often has the lowest entry prices thanks to welcome promos. Over time, count about €5 to €8 per serving depending on the number of recipes and people.

What is the best alternative to HelloFresh?

The main alternatives are Quitoque (more local and seasonal), Les Commis (chef-designed recipes) and Seazon (ready-to-heat meals). Our comparator puts them head to head.

How does a meal kit to cook work?

You pick your recipes, we deliver the fresh, pre-measured ingredients with the recipe cards, and you cook in 20 to 40 minutes. No shopping, no waste.

Is a meal kit box worth it?

Yes if you are short on time or ideas. It is a little pricier than the supermarket, but you save time, vary your recipes and cut waste thanks to right-sized portions.
