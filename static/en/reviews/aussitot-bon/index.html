Aussitôt Bon review: is the pre-cooked weekly box worth it?

Our Aussitôt Bon review after testing: a week of pre-cooked meals in one box, low-temperature cooking, price per dish and delivery under scrutiny.

By Benjamin Bourgoin

The Aussitôt Bon promise fits in one line: the week's meal box, already cooked, ready in 3 minutes. No peeling, no recipe card, you open, reheat, eat. What the plate is worth is the whole point of this Aussitôt Bon review after several weeks of testing.

How does Aussitôt Bon work?

Aussitôt Bon applies the weekly all-ready recipe: you compose your week among 20+ dishes on the menu, and everything arrives in a single refrigerated delivery. Dishes keep chilled and pass through the microwave in 3 minutes. The house signature is low-temperature cooking: dishes cooked by chefs then chilled to preserve textures and nutrients, a positioning in frontal competition with Seazon and Kitchen Daily.

Our detailed review after testing

Taste and quality: 7.5/10

Low temperature is no gimmick: meats come out tender, the pork tenderloin and the homemade couscous do the job, and the vegetables keep some bite instead of ending as mush. The overall level is consistent, which is precious on a subscription.

Menu variety: 6.0/10

More than 20 dishes each week, including vegetarian options like the chickpea curry: the count is there on paper. In use, the culinary universe stays tightened around French and Mediterranean classics, and regulars will see their favourites come back.

Convenience and flexibility: 7.5/10

One order, one delivery, one settled week: the formula removes the daily "what do we eat tonight". The 3 reheating minutes are kept, trays clean and dated, the subscription pauses easily. Half a point off for order modification, closed a bit early before delivery.

Delivery and customer service: 7.0/10

The refrigerated parcel arrived complete and on time across all our tests, worth highlighting. Customer service answers quickly with a human tone, and the brand proudly claims 4.9/5 over 1,000+ reviews. The delivery network stays less dense than the giants, to check for your town.

Value for money: 6.5/10

Outside promo, the meal starts at EUR 6.90 and climbs with the dishes: upper-average market, at Seazon level. The discovery offer at EUR 4.90 per meal clearly softens the first order. To put these rates against the rest of the market, see our guide on how much a meal kit costs.

The advantages and drawbacks of Aussitôt Bon

The advantages:

  • Low-temperature cooking, a real difference on the palate.
  • A full week of meals in a single delivery.
  • Dishes ready in 3 minutes, clean dated trays.
  • A responsive customer service, praised by reviews.

The drawbacks:

  • An upper-average price per meal outside promo.
  • A culinary universe a bit narrow over time.
  • Order modification closed early.

Aussitôt Bon, who is it for?

Aussitôt Bon suits anyone wanting to eat balanced without cooking, settling the week in one go: remote workers, busy families, office lunches. On the same ground, our Seazon review covers the pioneer of the genre. And if you prefer to cook fresh produce yourself, our Quitoque review remains the box-to-cook reference.

What customers say

With 4.9/5 displayed over 1,000+ reviews, customers are enthusiastic: dishes genuinely good rather than just fine, low temperature that changes everything, a week settled in 10 minutes of ordering. Reserves mostly concern the budget over time and the wish for more frequent menu novelties.

Our score: 3.5 / 5

Aussitôt Bon cleanly executes a simple promise: a week's meal box already cooked, which eats well thanks to low temperature. The rate outside promo and the tightened menu keep it away from the podium, but on the all-ready segment, one of the strongest files of the moment.

FAQ: your questions answered

How are Aussitôt Bon dishes cooked?

Dishes are cooked by chefs at low temperature, then chilled and delivered fresh. This method preserves textures and nutrients.

How much does an Aussitôt Bon meal cost?

Meals start at EUR 6.90, with a frequent discovery offer at EUR 4.90 per meal on the first order, sometimes with an extra discount code.

How long do the dishes keep?

Dishes keep several days in the fridge, with a use-by date on each tray. The box covers a week of meals.

Does Aussitôt Bon deliver everywhere in France?

Refrigerated delivery covers most of metropolitan France with reliable slots. Check your postcode at ordering.

Can you try Aussitôt Bon without commitment?

Yes, the subscription is commitment-free and the discovery offer lets you test the box at a reduced price before continuing, pausing or stopping.
