HelloFresh review: is the box really worth it?

Our review of HelloFresh after testing: recipe choice, price, freshness and flexibility. Does the world leader deliver?

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Reviews
  3. HelloFresh review: is the box really worth it?

HelloFresh is the world's number one meal kit. Huge catalogue, aggressive promotions: the box appeals to newcomers. But once the discount is gone, what is left? Our review after testing.

An unbeatable recipe choice

That is the strong point. Every week the catalogue is very wide, with enough to vary and please the whole family. Onboarding is simple and the recipe cards are well made.

Freshness and packaging

Ingredients are decent but more standardised than some rivals, with less marked seasonality. There is also quite a lot of packaging.

  • Very wide catalogue.
  • Attractive first-order price.
  • Available everywhere.

Price and flexibility

The welcome price is among the lowest, but it rises afterwards. Commitment-free, with free pause and cancellation. To compare, read our Quitoque review and the meal kit comparison.

Our score: 3.8 / 5

An excellent entry point to start, as long as you watch the price once the promo ends.

Is HelloFresh really cheaper?

The first order, yes, thanks to the promos. Over time the gap with rivals narrows.
