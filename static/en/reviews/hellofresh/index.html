HelloFresh review: is the box really worth it?

Our HelloFresh review after testing: recipe choice, price per serving, freshness and flexibility. Does the world leader deliver?

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Reviews
  3. HelloFresh review: is the box really worth it?
In short

HelloFresh is the ideal gateway into the world of meal kits to cook. Its catalogue is huge, its recipes are easy, and the welcome offer is very aggressive (up to several dozen euros off the first order). In return, the products are a little less local than Quitoque, more packaged, and the price per serving rises once the promo is over. Score: 3.8/5.

Welcome to our no-filter HelloFresh review. We ordered, paid for our baskets, and cooked the HelloFresh recipes for several weeks to see whether the world number one meal kit deserves its reputation. Here is our take, on the cooking side as well as the wallet side.

To place HelloFresh against other brands, use our comparator of the best meal kit boxes.

How does HelloFresh work?

The setup is clear. Each week you pick your dishes from a very wide catalogue, then receive a box with the products packaged and measured, with clear recipe cards. You then cook in 20 to 40 minutes. The brand offers several plans per week: 2 to 5 recipes, for 2 to 4 people. Everything is simple, built to save time. Like most players, the subscription is commitment-free.

Our detailed review after testing

Taste and quality: 7.0/10

The good basics always work and the dishes we cooked matched the photos. What keeps the score down: fresh produce that is correct but more standardised than local-first brands, and individually packaged vegetables that generate waste.

Menu variety: 9.0/10

This is where HelloFresh crushes the competition. The catalogue is gigantic, renewed each week, with enough to vary and please the whole family. The HelloFresh recipes are easy to follow, well illustrated, and the good basics always work. For beginners, it is reassuring: you cook simple, you rarely fail.

Convenience and flexibility: 8.0/10

Recipe cards are clear, ingredients pre-measured, and you cook in 20 to 40 minutes. The subscription is commitment-free: skip a week, change recipes or cancel in two clicks. We only dock a point because you still need to validate your menu before the weekly deadline.

Delivery and customer service: 7.5/10

Delivery is reliable and covers most of the country, an advantage over more regional brands. Parcels arrive well insulated, and support refunds a missing item quickly.

Value for money: 6.0/10

Let us talk money. The first order is very attractive thanks to the welcome offer. But once the first promo passes, the price per serving rises to around EUR 5 to 7 per serving depending on the plan. Before committing, look at the real cost per portion rather than the headline purchase price. We detail the method in our guide on how much a meal kit costs.

The advantages and drawbacks of HelloFresh

The advantages:

  • A huge catalogue, perfect to cook varied without getting bored.
  • Easy, well-explained recipes, ideal to start.
  • An unbeatable first-order offer.
  • Available everywhere, reliable delivery.
  • Commitment-free.

The drawbacks:

  • The price per serving that rises after the promo.
  • Products less local and seasonal than rivals.
  • A lot of packaged items and waste.

Welcome offer, refund and cashback

No honest HelloFresh review can skip the welcome offer, since that is what attracts most new customers. The first order gets a big discount, sometimes spread over several baskets. You also regularly find cashback codes via partner platforms. On refund, the brand is rather accommodating: a missing or damaged item leads to a quick refund. Before you pass an order, check the conditions, as the offer is mostly worth it the first time.

A typical HelloFresh week

To make it concrete, here are the recipes proposed during one test week, in a 3-dish plan for 2:

  • Honey roast chicken, seasonal vegetables and bulgur, ready in 30 minutes.
  • Creamy mushroom pasta, a comforting and super simple classic.
  • Spiced beef tacos to discover, with an express homemade sauce.

Each recipe comes with its fresh ingredients and foods already measured. We enjoyed discovering varied recipes without ever cooking the same thing twice, which breaks the evening routine. That is what HelloFresh meals do well: vary without effort.

Our tips to make the most of HelloFresh

  • Use the first order fully. The welcome offer is the real good deal.
  • Watch the price after the promo. That is when the cost per serving rises.
  • Play on volume. More recipes and people means a lower price per serving.
  • Pause without hesitation. It is the whole point of commitment-free.
  • Stack discounts. Think cashback codes and referral.

HelloFresh against the competition

In our ranking, HelloFresh is not first, but stays a safe value. Quitoque leads on freshness and local, Les Commis on chef recipes, and ready-meal boxes like Seazon target another need. Where HelloFresh keeps the edge is the choice of recipes proposed each week and geographic coverage. Compare side by side on our meal kit comparator.

FAQ: your questions answered

Is HelloFresh really cheaper?

The first order, yes, thanks to promos. Over time, count around EUR 5 to 7 per serving, and the gap with rivals narrows.

Are HelloFresh recipes easy?

Yes, they are designed to be simple and easy, with detailed cards. One of the most accessible boxes for beginners.

Can you choose your dishes each week?

Yes. Each week you build your menu from the catalogue before delivery.

Are the products fresh?

The fresh produce is correct but more standardised than local brands. It arrives packaged individually.

How to cancel or pause HelloFresh?

To cancel, deactivate from your account or contact support. Cancellation is free, and a refund is possible on a problem order.

Is HelloFresh worth it day to day?

Yes if you lack time or ideas. You save time, vary recipes and limit waste. A good purchase as long as you watch the post-promo price.
