Quitoque review: our full take after testing

Our review of Quitoque after testing: produce quality, recipes, price and subscription flexibility. The honest verdict.

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Reviews
  3. Quitoque review: our full take after testing

Quitoque comes up in every conversation about French meal kits. We wanted to check whether the reputation holds once the boxes are opened. Here is our verdict after several weeks of testing.

Fresh produce that keeps its promise

First impression: produce quality is there. The vegetables are firm, seasonal, and the French origin is highlighted without empty talk. Quantities are calibrated to avoid waste, a real plus day to day.

Varied, easy recipes

The weekly choice is wide, with options for every profile, vegetarian included. The recipe cards are clear and the prep times realistic.

  • Balanced, easy-to-follow recipes.
  • A good mix of discovery and comforting dishes.
  • Right-sized portions, few leftovers.

Price and flexibility

The price sits in the upper-middle of the market, justified by the quality. The subscription pauses easily, commitment-free. To place it, see our best meal kit comparison.

Our score: 4.6 / 5

Quitoque ticks the right boxes for anyone who wants to cook fresh and local without constraints.

Is Quitoque suitable for vegetarians?

Yes, vegetarian recipes are offered every week alongside the classic menus.

Can you choose your recipes?

Yes, you pick your dishes from the weekly catalogue before each delivery.
