Seazon review: our take on the ready-to-heat meals

Our review of Seazon after testing: ready-to-heat meals, nutritional balance, price and flexibility. Who is this box for?

By The Meilleure Box Repas team
  1. Home
  2. Reviews
  3. Seazon review: our take on the ready-to-heat meals

Seazon bets on balanced individual meals, reheated in a few minutes. A formula made for busy schedules. Our review after testing.

The convenience bet

No cooking, no washing up: you reheat and it is ready. The meals are balanced and the individual format is ideal for people living alone or for lunch at the office.

What we take away

  • Zero prep, just reheat.
  • Handy individual format.
  • Narrower choice than meal kits to cook.

Price and flexibility

The subscription pauses easily. The convenience-to-price ratio is fair, but you do not cook yourself. To compare with a cook-fresh box, see our Quitoque review.

Our score: 3.6 / 5

A good compromise to save time, provided you accept not cooking.

Are Seazon meals fresh?

They are prepared meals to reheat, designed to stay good, but less fresh than a home-cooked dish.
