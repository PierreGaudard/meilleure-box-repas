Seazon review: our test of the ready-to-heat meals

Our Seazon review after testing: fresh ready-to-heat meals, nutritional balance, price, promo and customer service. Who is this box for?

By Benjamin Bourgoin
  1. Home
  2. Reviews
  3. Seazon review: our test of the ready-to-heat meals
In short

Seazon bets on individual fresh meals, cooked by chefs and simply reheated. It is the ideal solution for anyone who does not want to cook but wants to eat balanced. Delivery is reliable, the dishes are healthy and of good quality, the customer service responsive. Against it: a narrower choice than a box to cook, and a slightly higher price per plate. Score: 3.6/5.

How does Seazon work?

The principle is radically different from a box to cook. You choose your dishes from a changing menu, place your order, and a few days later you receive fresh meals already cooked, to keep in the fridge and reheat in minutes. No leftovers, no washing up, no shopping. Seazon offers monthly or per-order plans, depending on your needs, commitment-free.

Our detailed review after testing

Taste and quality: 8.0/10

Good surprise: the Seazon meals are tasty and far from the sad industrial tray. The recipes are varied, rather healthy, with real work on nutritional balance. These are fresh meals, not frozen, which shows in the taste even after reheating. We enjoyed discovering recipes we would not have cooked ourselves. Some textures stay a notch below homemade, hence 8 and not more.

Menu variety: 6.5/10

The menu changes regularly and covers classics as well as more original recipes, with vegetarian options. But the choice stays narrower than a box to cook like HelloFresh: over several weeks of orders, the same dishes come back. That is our main reproach.

Convenience and flexibility: 9.5/10

This is the absolute strong point. You pick your dishes, you reheat, you eat: no shopping, no prep dishes. The app lets you pause, skip a week or cancel in a few clicks, commitment-free. Hard to make simpler.

Delivery and customer service: 6.5/10

Delivery is serious: insulated parcel, cold chain respected, clear slots. We remove points for the carrier, sometimes less punctual depending on the area, but customer service answers fast and refunds without arguing.

Value for money: 5.5/10

Let us talk money. The price per plate is a bit higher than a box to cook, which is logical: here the cooking is done. Count on average EUR 6 to 9 per plate depending on the plan. Good news: Seazon regularly offers a welcome promo and a discount code on the first order. To compare with the rest of the market, see our guide on how much a meal kit costs.

Storage, vegetables and practical info

A few concrete points about these cooked dishes. Storage is simple: the dishes keep several days in the fridge, with a clear date on each tray. The vegetable portions are decent, making the meals more filling than feared. Above a certain order amount, free delivery applies, a detail that counts on the monthly budget. That said, for a truly complete weekly menu, you sometimes need to add a side. Seazon also occasionally offers gifts or loyalty perks.

The advantages and drawbacks of Seazon

The advantages:

  • Dishes ready to reheat, zero prep.
  • Fresh meals, healthy and rather balanced.
  • Reliable delivery and good customer service.
  • Commitment-free, with promo and code on the first order.

The drawbacks:

  • A higher price per plate than a box to cook.
  • A narrower choice on the menu.
  • You do not cook yourself.

Seazon, who is it for?

Seazon is perfect for anyone who wants to eat healthily without cooking: busy people, people living alone, office lunches. If on the contrary you like to cook and look for fresh products to prepare yourself, look at a box to cook like our Quitoque review. And to start at the lowest price, our HelloFresh review remains a reference. If your goal is weight loss, look at Cheef, specialised in slimming cooked dishes.

Our score: 3.6 / 5

Seazon keeps its promises for anyone seeking fresh meals ready to reheat, healthy and well delivered. We remove points for the price per plate and a more limited choice, but the service genuinely helps day to day. A good time/quality compromise, as long as you accept not cooking.

FAQ: your questions answered

Are Seazon dishes fresh?

Yes, they are fresh meals cooked by chefs, kept in the fridge and reheated. Never frozen.

How does Seazon delivery work?

You place an order, and a few days later the dishes arrive in an insulated parcel. Delivery is reliable.

Is there a Seazon promo code?

Yes, Seazon regularly offers a promo and a discount code on the first order.

Is Seazon expensive?

The price per plate (EUR 6 to 9 depending on the plan) is higher than a box to cook, because the cooking is done for you.

How to cancel Seazon easily?

The subscription is commitment-free: pause, change or cancel anytime, no fees, from your customer account.
